The Great Gatsby

F. Scott Fitzgerald • 1925

SUMMARY

A tragic critique of the American Dream, set in the roaring 1920s on Long Island. The story follows Nick Carraway as he becomes entangled in the life of the mysterious Jay Gatsby and his obsessive pursuit of Daisy Buchanan, his former lover.

Character Relationships
